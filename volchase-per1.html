FAQ

Straight answers.

What is VolChase? +
VolChase is a live trading competition platform. Connect your CEX account, join a campaign, and race other traders on volume. Top ranks earn from the prize pool — daily.
Is my exchange account safe? +
Yes. Read-only API key only. We see your trading volume — we touch nothing else. Your funds stay on your exchange, under your control.
How does ranking work? +
Volume = rank. We track the trading volume of the specified token pair on your connected exchange. The more you trade, the higher your rank. Transparent, on-chain verifiable, manipulation-resistant.
How do I claim rewards? +
Rewards are sent automatically to your DeFi wallet address on Arbitrum One within 24 hours after each epoch ends. No manual claim needed.
Which exchanges are supported? +
Binance, Bybit, OKX, Kraken, KuCoin, Gate.io, Bitget, MEXC, Crypto.com, Coinbase, and Huobi/HTX. New exchanges added regularly.
